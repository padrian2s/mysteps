MySteps tracks your steps every hour and reminds you to move when you haven't reached your goal. Designed for people who sit for long periods and need regular movement reminders.

FEATURES

Step Counter Complication
• Shows your hourly step count directly on your watch face
• Progress arc fills up as you walk toward your goal
• Heart icon appears when you reach your hourly goal
• Daily progress shows how many hours you've completed (e.g. 3/5)
• Updates every 2 seconds when the screen is on

Vibration Alarm
• Vibrates 10 minutes before the hour if you haven't reached your step goal
• Strong repeating vibration pattern — impossible to miss
• Notification appears with your current step count
• Works reliably even when the watch screen is off

Fully Configurable
• Step goal: adjust in increments of 50 (default: 250 steps per hour)
• Active hours: set your start and end time (default: 8 AM to 9 PM)
• Alarm duration: choose how long the vibration lasts
• Toggle alarm on/off

HOW TO USE

1. Install MySteps on your Wear OS watch
2. Long-press your watch face → Customize → select a complication slot
3. Choose "Hourly Steps" from the list
4. Open MySteps app to adjust your step goal and alarm settings
5. Walk! The complication updates in real-time

PRIVACY

MySteps runs entirely on your watch. No data is collected, sent to servers, or shared with anyone. All step data stays on your device.

REQUIREMENTS

• Wear OS 3.0 or later
• Watch with step counter sensor (Pixel Watch, Galaxy Watch, etc.)
